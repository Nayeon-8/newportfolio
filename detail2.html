FOODHOOD

Community app connecting neighbors to share and trade homemade meals.

Foodhood combines "Food" and "Hood" to create a community-based platform where local residents can safely and reliably share and trade homemade meals. In today’s society, where neighborly connections are diminishing, Foodhood provides a warm space for fostering local relationships and showcasing dishes lovingly prepared by home chefs.

Through a friendly and intuitive design, the platform promotes a culture of sharing and communication beyond simple transactions, helping users easily discover the heartfelt taste and nostalgic memories of home-cooked meals.
