CONVERSE

Character illustration series for CONVERSE merchandise, blending sports and futuristic technology themes.

This illustration project was created for the iconic sportswear brand CONVERSE. The concept explores the fusion of sports and technology through a set of four personalized characters, each wearing CONVERSE products. Inspired by dynamic athletic poses, the illustrations reflect energy, movement, and individuality—capturing the brand’s spirit in a futuristic context.
