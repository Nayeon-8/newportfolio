Aetherspire

Character illustration series for CONVERSE merchandise, blending sports and futuristic technology themes.

Aetherspire is an innovative floating skyscraper designed with AI, embodying the future of sustainable urban living.

The building's exterior and styling were created using ChatGPT and Getimg AI. By integrating nature with advanced technology, it establishes a self-sustaining ecosystem, with the upper levels hovering in misty air and the lower sections forming a vertical forest intertwined with massive tree roots and vines. Its facade, covered with moss and algae, simultaneously purifies the air and generates solar energy.

Inside, sunlit atriums house indoor gardens that provide a comfortable air environment and restful spaces, serving as community hubs for residents. The rooftop features a forest that promotes biodiversity, offering a sanctuary for both wildlife and inhabitants.

Powered by levitation technology utilizing wind, solar energy, and atmospheric moisture, it exemplifies an eco-friendly and innovative design model. This architectural design was selected as a Top 10 finalist in the Asia-wide ‘Reimagine The Future of Hospitality with AI’ competition, earning an opportunity for formal presentation.
