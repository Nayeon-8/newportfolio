Omniversity

Design and development of a multifunctional community app using React Native

This project is a community-based mobile application that began in April 2025 through collaboration with Korean students currently studying at NUS and those with exchange program experience. With the concept of “the universe,” the app is designed to help university students—especially those in unfamiliar environments like exchange students—adapt more easily by providing practical guides and offering a platform to freely share school events and information.

As a product designer and frontend developer, I led the overall design, created all visual elements, and participated in the frontend development using React Native. Through this project, I’ve gained hands-on experience in both user-centered interface design and real-world app development.
