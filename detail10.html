SUBWAY TO DREAMS

A illustration created with Clip Studio, featuring beloved Kaleidoscope characters

This illustration was created using Clip Studio, a digital drawing program. It was submitted to an unofficial competition where it won a bronze medal. The artwork features characters I like from the webtoon Kaleidoscope, depicted riding the subway. Each character has a unique personality, making it enjoyable to focus on them individually.

A key aspect of the illustration is that the subway is traveling toward their home—the kaleidoscope—where they provide dreams and hope to the passengers. To convey this sense of fantasy and aspiration, the subway interior is adorned with elements like shimmering pink water, floating clouds, and sparkling soap bubbles. The pink sunset sky complements the pink exterior of the subway, enhancing the dreamy atmosphere.
